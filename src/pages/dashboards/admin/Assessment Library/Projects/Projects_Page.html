About Projects

Projects are in-depth assessments that require students to apply multiple financial literacy concepts to real-world scenarios. They develop critical thinking and practical application skills.

Pre-configured projects are available for each standard, or you can create custom projects to meet your specific teaching objectives.

Project Implementation Tips

  • Provide clear rubrics with specific evaluation criteria
  • Break projects into milestones with checkpoint due dates
  • Include both individual and group components when possible
  • Leverage AI-assisted grading for consistent evaluation

AI Support Information

Projects utilize AI to provide students with guidance and immediate feedback throughout the completion process. AI support includes:

  • Real-time feedback on analysis components
  • Calculation verification and error guidance
  • Conceptual explanations when students are stuck
  • Consistency verification across project components
