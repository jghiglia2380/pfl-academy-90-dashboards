Financial Services Evaluation Project

Standard 3: Financial Service Providers and Account Management

45 Minutes Written Analysis Calculations Required

In this capstone project, you will evaluate and compare different financial service providers to make informed recommendations based on specific client needs and circumstances. You'll analyze fees, services, accessibility, and technology features to determine the best fit for various scenarios.

Required Materials:

  • Calculator
  • Word processor or paper for written responses
  • Spreadsheet software (optional but recommended)
