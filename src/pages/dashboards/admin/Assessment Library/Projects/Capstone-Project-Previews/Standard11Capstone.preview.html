Protecting What Matters: Your Risk and Insurance Plan

Standard 11: Understanding Risk Management and Insurance

45 Minutes Written Plan Basic Math

In this project, you'll create a practical plan to protect yourself and your family from everyday risks. You'll learn how insurance works, figure out what coverage you need, and make a simple plan to stay protected. Don't worry - we'll break it down into easy steps!

What You'll Need:

  • Basic calculator
  • Something to write with
  • Your thinking cap!

Your Tasks

Task 1: Spot the Risks (15 minutes)

Let's identify what could go wrong and how big of a deal it might be

  • Make a list of possible problems (like car accidents or injuries)
  • Rate how likely they are to happen (1-3 scale)
  • Think about how bad it would be if they happened
  • Circle the ones that worry you most

Task 2: Insurance Check (15 minutes)

Figure out what insurance you need and what it might cost

  • Match each risk to a type of insurance
  • Look at basic insurance costs
  • Find what's missing in current coverage
  • Pick what's most important to get first

Task 3: Make Your Plan (15 minutes)

Create a simple plan to protect yourself and your stuff

  • List your top 3 protection needs
  • Write down when you'll get each one
  • Figure out how much to save each month
  • Plan how to check if it's working
