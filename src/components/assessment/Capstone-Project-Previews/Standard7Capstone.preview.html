Sample Monthly Payment Calculation

For a $20,000 loan at 6.5% APR for 48 months:

1. Monthly Payment Formula: PMT = P × (r × (1 + r)^n) ÷ ((1 + r)^n - 1)

Where:

  • P = Principal ($20,000)
  • r = Monthly interest rate (6.5% ÷ 12 = 0.5417%)
  • n = Total number of months (48)

2. Monthly Payment = $474.91

3. Total Interest = (Monthly Payment × Number of Months) - Principal

4. Total Interest = ($474.91 × 48) - $20,000 = $2,795.68
