Project Tasks

Task 1: Organization Comparison (15 minutes)

Compare two youth education organizations using their annual reports.

  • • Review annual reports and financial statements
  • • Compare program outcomes and reach
  • • Evaluate cost per student served
  • • Assess organizational stability

Task 2: Personal Giving Plan (15 minutes)

Create a monthly giving plan with $5,000 annual budget:

  • • Plan monthly contribution schedule
  • • Calculate tax deduction benefits
  • • Balance giving with emergency savings
  • • Consider volunteer time commitments

Task 3: Community Impact (15 minutes)

Evaluate the impact of your giving plan:

  • • Estimate number of students benefited
  • • Project program hours funded
  • • Calculate cost-effectiveness
  • • Assess long-term community benefits
