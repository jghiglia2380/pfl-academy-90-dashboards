Consumer Protection & Identity Theft Prevention Project

Standard 9: Consumer Protection

45 Minutes Written Analysis Calculations Required

In this capstone project, you will analyze real-world fraud scenarios, identify potential threats, and develop comprehensive protection strategies. You'll demonstrate your ability to recognize common scams, protect personal information, and create effective response plans for various security threats.

Required Materials:

  • Computer with internet access
  • Digital document for responses
  • Security checklist template (provided)

Project Tasks

Fraud Detection Analysis (15 minutes)

Analyze potential fraud scenarios and identify red flags

  • Review suspicious email communication
  • Analyze social media investment scam
  • Identify specific warning signs
  • Document potential consequences
  • Propose prevention strategies

Identity Theft Prevention (15 minutes)

Evaluate identity theft risks and develop protection measures

  • Assess personal information vulnerabilities
  • Review data breach scenario
  • Create security enhancement plan
  • Recommend monitoring tools
  • Outline preventive measures

Response Planning (15 minutes)

Develop comprehensive response plans for security incidents

  • Create incident response timeline
  • List immediate action steps
  • Identify reporting authorities
  • Document recovery procedures
  • Develop future prevention strategies

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Multi-Channel Fraud Analysis

From: SecureBank Security Team <security-alert@secure-bank-verify.net>

To: Valued Customer

Subject: ⚠️ URGENT: Suspicious Activity Detected

Dear Valued Customer,

We have detected unusual activity on your SecureBank account. For your protection, your account access has been temporarily limited.

Click here to verify your identity and restore access: [LINK]

@TechSupport_Help

2 hours ago

Having account issues? Our support team is here 24/7! DM us your account details and we'll help restore access immediately.

Analysis Questions:

  1. Identify at least 3 red flags in the email and social media post that indicate potential fraud.
  2. Explain how these communications attempt to exploit common psychological triggers.
  3. Outline the proper steps for verifying legitimate bank communications.

Scenario 3: Data Breach Response

Breach Notification:

A major retailer has reported a data breach affecting 100,000 customers. The compromised data includes:

  • • Full names and addresses
  • • Credit card numbers (last 4 digits only)
  • • Purchase history from the past 6 months
  • • Email addresses and phone numbers

Response Plan Development:

  1. Create a prioritized checklist of immediate actions for affected customers.
  2. Develop a 90-day monitoring and protection plan.
  3. Design a template for documenting suspicious activity and communications with authorities.
