Credit and Borrowing Analysis Project

Standard 4: Credit and Borrowing Decisions

45 Minutes Written Analysis Calculations Required

In this capstone project, you will demonstrate your mastery of credit management and borrowing decisions through comprehensive analysis, critical thinking, and strategic planning. You will evaluate real-world scenarios, perform financial calculations, and develop evidence-based recommendations.

Required Materials:

  • Calculator
  • Word processor or paper for written responses
  • Spreadsheet software (optional but recommended)

Project Tasks and Deliverables

Part 1: Credit Product Analysis (15 minutes)

Compare and analyze three different credit products:

Scenario:

You are advising three different clients on their credit options:

  • Client A: College student looking for their first credit card
  • Client B: Family looking to finance a $25,000 car purchase
  • Client C: Homebuyer considering a $300,000 mortgage

Required Deliverables:

  • Complete a detailed comparison matrix for each scenario including:
    • Interest rates and APR calculations
    • Total cost analysis (interest, fees, insurance)
    • Required credit score and income requirements
    • Risk assessment for both lender and borrower
  • Show all calculations used in your analysis
  • Provide specific product recommendations with justification

Part 2: Financial Impact Analysis (15 minutes)

Case Study:

The Thompson family is considering a home renovation:

  • Monthly household income: $6,500
  • Current monthly debt payments: $2,000
  • Renovation cost: $45,000
  • Current credit score: 720

Required Deliverables:

  • Calculate and analyze:
    • Current debt-to-income ratio
    • Maximum affordable monthly payment
    • Impact on monthly cash flow
    • Break-even analysis for home value increase
  • Compare three financing options:
    • Home equity loan
    • Personal loan
    • Credit card with 0% APR promotion
  • Provide a detailed 24-month cash flow projection

Part 3: Strategic Recommendations (15 minutes)

Required Deliverables:

  • Develop a comprehensive recommendation for the Thompson family including:
    • Optimal financing choice with justification
    • Risk mitigation strategies
    • Alternative scenarios analysis
    • Impact on long-term financial goals
  • Create an implementation timeline including:
    • Credit application process
    • Required documentation
    • Key milestones and decision points
    • Contingency plans

Submission Requirements

Format Requirements:

  • All calculations must be shown and clearly labeled
  • Written analysis must be clear, concise, and professional
  • Include citations for any external data or resources used
  • Organize your work using clear headings and sections

Required Components:

  • Completed comparison matrices for all scenarios
  • Detailed calculations and financial projections
  • Written analysis and recommendations
  • Implementation timeline and risk mitigation strategies
